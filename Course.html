Meet your instructor

Miss Angela will take you step by step through a location photo shoot,
show both lo-fi and high-production budget approaches to a fashion
set-up, walk you through her post production process and share her
insight into the business of fashion photography.

You will learn how to concept, produce, and style a shoot — including
finding inspirational details and creative locations. After reflecting on the
shoot and reviewing the raw images, Miss Angela will walk you through
her compositing process. You will learn how to choose images that
both highlight your personal style and appeal to stylists, editors, and
commercial clients.
